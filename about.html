Government Primary School, Chhindibirhi is a Government-run school under the School Education Department, Government of Chhattisgarh. It was established in 1995 to provide free and quality primary education to all children in and around Chhindibirhi village. The school focuses on basic learning, discipline, cleanliness, and cultural values so that every child becomes confident and responsible.
